Footloose

The movie Footloose is a story of a teenage boy, Ren McCormack, played by Kevin Bacon. Ren moves to a small town from Chicago with his mom. He loves to dance and is a total fox, but the small town has banned dancing after a group of teenagers died in the way back from a night out dancing and drinking. While not everyone is a fan of Ren's big city moves, he makes friends with the awkward country boy, Willard, who he teaches to dance and quickly falls for the miniter's daugher. For a movie set in a town without dancing, there is plenty of awesome dance numbers you'll want to reenact with all your friends. They redid the movie in 2011 but it is not as awesome, but still entertaining, since it is based on an amazing movie.
